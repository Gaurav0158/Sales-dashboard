# Sales-dashboard
Smartphone Sales Analysis Dashboard | Power BI
“Built a Power BI dashboard to analyze smartphone sales with KPIs, monthly trends, city-wise insights, model performance, and payment method analysis

Key Responsibilities & Work Done:
* Power BI
* Power Query
* DAX
* Excel
* Data Modeling
* Data Cleaning & Transformation
* Data Visualization

* ### Data Source
- Smartphone sales data compiled through independent online research from multiple publicly available sources.

* Business Problem
- The business needed a clear understanding of smartphone sales performance across different cities, brands, models, and customer segments. 
Sales data was scattered, making it difficult to identify top-selling products, customer preferences, seasonal trends, and revenue drivers.

* Goal of the Dashboard
To build an interactive and insight-driven Power BI dashboard that helps stakeholders track sales performance, analyze trends across months, 
compare city-wise and model-wise sales, understand customer purchasing behavior, and make data-backed business decisions.

* Walkthrough of Key Visuals
- KPI Cards: Show total sales (₹769M), total quantity (19K), total transactions (4K), and average sales.
- Map Visual: Displays city-wise sales performance across major Indian cities.
- Monthly Trend Line Chart: Highlights monthly variation in quantity sold and detects sales peaks & drops.
- Brand/Model Bar Chart: Compares performance of different smartphone brands and top-selling models.
- Rating Funnel Chart: Shows customer satisfaction levels based on ratings.
- Payment Method Donut Chart: Analyzes preferred payment modes such as UPI, Credit Card, Debit Card, etc.
- Slicers: Allow filtering by Month, Brand, Payment Method, and Smartphone Model for deeper insights.

* Business Impact & Insights
- Identified top-performing cities that contribute most to sales revenue.
- Highlighted best-selling smartphone models and underperforming ones.
- Revealed monthly patterns showing periods of peak demand.
- Showed that digital payment methods like UPI dominate customer transactions.
- Customer ratings helped understand satisfaction trends and areas of improvement.
- Enabled leadership to make data-driven decisions related to inventory, marketing, and product focus.
